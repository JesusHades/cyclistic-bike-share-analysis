#Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate.

The objective was to analyze behavioral differences between casual riders and annual members of Cyclistic, a bike-share company, and provide data-driven recommendations to increase annual memberships.

#Business Task
Identify how annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently and propose strategies to convert casual riders into annual members.

#Tools Used
SQL (BigQuery)
Tableau Public
Google Sheets / CSV
Data Cleaning and Transformation

#Key Findings
Annual members account for approximately 73% of all rides.
Casual riders have longer average ride durations.
Annual members primarily ride during weekdays and commuting hours.
Casual riders show higher activity during weekends and afternoon hours.
Both groups strongly prefer electric bikes.

#Recommendations
Weekend Membership Campaign
Incentives for Frequent Casual Riders
Communicate Membership Value
